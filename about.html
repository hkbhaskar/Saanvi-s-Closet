About Saanvi's Closet

We provide Pakistani Designers, including Sana Safinaz, Baroque and Motifz, among others PAN India delivery at a nominal delivey charges as per your locaton distance.
Our fabrics are of 100% genuine quality passed by many fabric checks stitching quality check.




Our Philosophy

There’s beauty in diversity. So, we shelved the one-size-fits-all approach in favour of one that celebrates differences, of every kind.
Whatever her style, size, or spending power, we want each woman who shops with us to feel welcome, inspired and excited.
This is reflected in the very varied mix of over 500 labels and 750 styles that make up Sanvi Coset today.
Here, you can see lesser-known, luxe and high-street names sit together in harmony.

Our Mission

We aim to make shopping with us as seamless as possible. While we give you access to thousands of products across a
slew of categories at your fingertips, you won’t feel like you’re caught in clutter. We hold your hand through every step
of the way with easy-to-use filters, carefully created curations, expert insights from our in-house styles, content driven by
timely topics as well as an accessible customer service that really listens to what you have to say.

What We Do

We’ve worked hard towards building a team of seriously cool and talented people and we want you to know who they are.
You can get a glimpse of style and lives on our website, by following us on Facebook. We’ll know we’re successful not through
skyrocketing numbers but if we manage to build a community of customers who cares for us as deeply as we care for them.
Keep coming back to us and tell us what you love, there’s nothing that makes us happier!
